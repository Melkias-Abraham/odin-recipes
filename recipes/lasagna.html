Lasagna Recipe

Easy homemade lasagne featuring a blend of cheese, itlaian herbs and spices

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sacue
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stire in the spaghetti saice and simmer
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
