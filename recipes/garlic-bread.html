Garlic Bread Recipe

This is the BEST homemade garlic bread you'll ever make. It has fresh herbs, tons of flavorful roasted garlic, and is perfectly toasted. This easy garlic bread recipe is delicious served with your fav pasta dishes & comfort foods, and is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Cheese
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Combine softened butter, Parmesan cheese, and minced garlic in a bowl until well combined.
  2. Mix in garlic salt, Italian seasoning, pepper, and paprika until smooth and well combined.
  3. Spread garlic butter both sides of bread, pre heat oven to 400 degrees celisus
  4. Place bread in oven for 4 minutes, flip halfway through.
